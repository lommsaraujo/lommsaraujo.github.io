Mini Bio

Jasmin Cortez is a Brazilian-German actor and singer-songwriter. She started writing at the age of 9, releasing her single "Volta e Meia" a few years after. She had her theatre debut in Rio de Janeiro with Pentesiléia (2020). At 13, Jasmin started studying musical theatre and followed that starring in Frozen - Uma Aventura Musical (2018). During her drama degree, Cortez played in After the Fall (2018), Decomposed Theatre (2019), Aurora da Minha Vida (2019) and Estamos Progredindo (2019). Jasmin entered the Amsterdam theatre scene staring in Monstersongs! (2022) and Noir Suspicions (2022).
